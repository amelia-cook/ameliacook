Chocolate Walnut Oat Cookies

Ingredients

2 sticks of unsalted butter
3/4 cups white sugar
3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
2 large eggs
1 1/4 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoons lemon juice
2 1/4 cups flour
1/2 cups rolled oats
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
pinch cinnamon
2 2/3 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 1/2 cups chopped walnuts

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 300° Fahrenheit
  2. Cream butter, sugar, and brown sugar in a mixer on medium speed for about 2 minutes
  3. Add eggs, vanilla, and lemon juice, blending in mixer on low speed for about 30 seconds
  4. Mix on medium speed for about 2 minutes or until light and fluffy, scraping the bowl
  5. With mixer on low speed, add flour, oats, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon, blending for about 45 seconds
  6. Remove bowl from mixer and stir in chocolate chips and walnuts
  7. Cut dough into 3-tablespoon sized pieces and place onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, about 2 inches apart
  8. Bake for 20-23 minutes, until edges are golden brown and center is still soft
  9. Remove from oven and cool for about 1 hour

Notes

  • seeds from half a vanilla bean substitute for teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • dough freezes very well, can put directly into oven (though good idea to cut dough before freezing)
