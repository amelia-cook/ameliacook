Blueberry Buckle

Ingredients

3/4 cup white sugar
1/4 cup shortening
1 egg
1/2 cup milk
2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 cups fresh blueberries
1/2 cup white sugar
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 cup butter, softened

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375° Fahrenheit. Grease one 8x8 inch pan
  2. Cream together 3/4 cup sugar, shortening, and egg
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together 2 cups flour, baking powder, and salt
  4. Stir into sugar mixture, alternating with milk
  5. Stir in blueberries
  6. Pour into greased 8x8 pan
  7. To make topping, combine 1/2 cup sugar, 1/3 cup flour, cinnamon, and butter
  8. Sprinkle topping over cake batter
  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes

Notes

  • frozen blueberries work well too
